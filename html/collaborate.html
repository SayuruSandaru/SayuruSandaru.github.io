Collaborate with Us


Are you an artist looking to collaborate with B & S? We are always open to working with talented musicians and creating new, exciting music.

To be considered for a collaboration, please follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the form below with your name, email, and a brief message describing your proposal and why you would like to collaborate with us.
  2. Attach a link to your music or a sample of your work.
  3. Please note that due to the high volume of submissions we receive, we may not be able to respond to every inquiry. However, if we are interested in working with you, we will reach out to discuss further.

We look forward to hearing from you and possibly working together in the future!
